◆ resizeDistributionFactor

double jaut::SplitPanel::Options::resizeDistributionFactor = 0

Determines the pane that will get the extra space when resizing the content area.


A value of 0.0 means pane 2 will get all the new area, 1.0 means pane 1 will get all the new space.
Anything in-between will distribute the change for both panes depending on the pivot of 0.5 which will evenly distribute the size for both panels. (<separator position> += (+/-<size diff> * <distribution>))
